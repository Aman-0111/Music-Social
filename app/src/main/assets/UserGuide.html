Important Information


Music Social makes use of the Spotify API. There are differences in the app based on whether the device has the Spotify app installed or not. These differences will be pointed out and shown when necessary.


This User Guide is split into different pages. If there is a section of the app that you may have questions on, please refer to the navigation bar. The navigation bar has clearly labelled tabs dedicated to parts of the app, such as the Home Page, Recent Songs Page, Login Page and App Navigation.


NOTE: All pages have a portrait and landscape variation
