App Navigation Information


There are two main ways to navigate the Music Social. The first way is the Navigation Bar at the bottom of the screen.




Tapping the icons on the navigation bar will take the user into that section of the app. The second way in by swiping the screen. So if the user swipes from right to left on the home screen the app will display the recent tracks screen.


The share action provider in the app is displayed at all times and allows the user to share their Spotify profile. Share capabilities can be more limited on some devices than others.
